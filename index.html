ACM SIGIR Conference on

Human Information Interaction and Retrieval (CHIIR)




March 07-11, 2027
Berlin, Germany

Next Stop: Berlin

After Seattle, CHIIR heads to Berlin for five days of research, discussion, and community around human-information interaction and retrieval. Come for papers, posters, demos, tutorials, workshops, an industry session, and memorable social events. Stay a little longer for Berlin’s history and landmarks, neighborhoods and museums, food, music, and for good conversations.

Bring your ideas, and maybe a little extra time for Berlin.
