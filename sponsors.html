Call for Sponsorship ACM CHIIR

March 07-11, 2027
Berlin, Germany

Call for Sponsors and Exhibitors

On behalf of the Organizing Committee of the ACM SIGIR Conference on Human Information Interaction and Retrieval (CHIIR 2027) we would like to invite you to participate as a sponsor or exhibitor. CHIIR is the leading international forum dedicated to human interaction with information and knowledge systems. The CHIIR community includes the top researchers and thought leaders in academia and industry working at the intersection of AI and user interaction with information and knowledge.

Scientific Program

The CHIIR 2027 Committee is planning a stimulating program covering five days, with research papers, posters, tutorials, workshops, an industry day, and social events.

Delegate Profile

CHIIR rotates between North America, Europe, and Asia. We expect a broad mix of attendees with the majority of them from Europe, the US and Canada, China, Australia, and Japan. About half of the delegates are PhD students, many of whom will be seeking opportunities to shape the next generation of human-facing AI. Based on the reputation of the conference and the fact that CHIIR 2027 will be in Berlin (!) we expect about 200 delegates.

Benefits of Supporting

CHIIR 2027 Benefits include acknowledgement of your organization’s support and unique networking opportunities during the conference with researchers and graduate students. As a conference sponsor you will be recognised (with your logo) in the conference program and on the official conference web site. We offer general conference sponsorship and targeted sponsorship opportunities. General conference sponsors benefit from continuous visibility before and during the conference.

Other Sponsorship Opportunities

Additional sponsorship opportunities include funding of events and other aspects of the conference: the welcome reception, a poster reception, the main social feature: the conference banquet, lunches, the student volunteer program, and many more. We also offer a wide range of in-kind sponsorship opportunities (just reach out to us and we can explore what suits you best).

Contact

If you are interested in discussing our sponsorship options in more detail or if you would like to explore further sponsorship opportunities tailored to your own business, then please contact us at chiir2027sponsorship@lists.hu-berlin.de (or directly via the Sponsorship Chair: udo.kruschwitz@ur.de).
